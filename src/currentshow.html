EW TRISTRAM: THE EASTBOURNE PANELS

Private View: Friday 3 November, 6pm
Exhibition: 4 November–17 December, 12 noon–5pm (Thurs–Suns)

Closing Early at 2pm on Saturday 4 November for Lewes Bonfire
Closing Late at 9pm on Thursday 7 December for Lewes Late Night Shopping

Never before seen in public, Martyrs’ Gallery is delighted to present the exquisite Eastbourne Panels, created in 1938 by art historian and restorer Ernest William Tristram and gifted to St Elisabeth’s Church in Eastbourne, on whose behalf we are fundraising.

Please join us for a range of special events listed at www.martyrs.gallery/upcomingevents and donate generously to this cause, which will support valuable arts initiatives as well as the local community.

The gallery will be available to book for study days on EW Tristrams’s work throughout November and December for schools and other groups. Please email info@martyrs.gallery for availability and prices.

Read the extraordinary story of the Panels' discovery in November’s Viva Lewes: http://www.vivabrighton.com/viva-lewes (p55).
